Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by equal your total score to random target.

You lose the game if your total score goes above the random target.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
